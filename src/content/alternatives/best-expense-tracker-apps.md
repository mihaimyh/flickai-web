## What We Looked For: The Criteria That Actually Matter

Before diving into specific recommendations, let's establish the evaluation framework. The best expense tracker apps excel in these areas:

### Fast Capture: Speed and Accuracy

The core value of any expense tracker is how quickly and accurately it can capture expense data. This means:
- **Receipt scanning speed:** Can you snap a photo and move on, or do you need to wait for processing?
- **OCR accuracy:** Does the app correctly read faded receipts, handwritten notes, and various receipt formats?
- **Data extraction quality:** Does it pull the right merchant name, date, total, and line items?
- **Batch processing:** Can you scan multiple receipts efficiently, or is it one-at-a-time tedium?

### Offline Mode and Reliable Syncing

Connectivity isn't guaranteed. Whether you're on a flight, in a remote client location, or experiencing network issues, your expense tracker must work. Look for:
- **Complete offline functionality:** Full feature access without internet connectivity
- **Reliable sync:** Data syncs correctly when connectivity returns, with no conflicts or data loss
- **Offline receipt scanning:** Can capture and process receipts without internet
- **Conflict resolution:** Handles sync conflicts intelligently when data changes offline and online

### Privacy-First Options

Not everyone wants (or can have) their expense data flowing through cloud services with mandatory bank account connections. Privacy-first options offer:
- **On-device processing:** Sensitive data processed locally, not in the cloud
- **Optional cloud sync:** You control when and how data syncs
- **No mandatory bank connections:** Expense tracking works without connecting bank accounts
- **Transparent data policies:** Clear information about where data is stored and how it's used

### Pricing You Can Start With

Subscription fatigue is real. The best expense tracker apps offer:
- **Free to start:** Genuinely functional free tiers, not just teasers
- **Transparent pricing:** Clear, upfront costs without hidden fees
- **Reasonable scaling:** Pricing that scales with usage, not arbitrary user limits
- **No surprise charges:** Essential features aren't locked behind premium paywalls
