## What We Looked For: The Criteria That Actually Matter

Before diving into specific apps, let's establish what separates excellent receipt scanners from merely functional ones:

### OCR Accuracy: The Foundation

**Optical Character Recognition (OCR)** is the technology that reads text from receipt images. Accuracy rates matter enormously:
- **95%+ accuracy:** Industry-leading performance, minimal corrections needed
- **90-94% accuracy:** Good performance, occasional manual corrections
- **Below 90% accuracy:** Frustrating user experience, frequent corrections needed

**Real-World Test:** We tested each app with:
- Faded receipts (6+ months old, faded thermal paper)
- Handwritten notes on receipts
- Long grocery receipts (50+ line items)
- International receipts (different formats, currencies)
- Crumpled receipts (pulled from wallets, slightly damaged)

### Offline-Friendly Capture

Receipt scanning often happens when connectivity is unreliable—on planes, in remote client locations, or during network outages. The best receipt scanners offer:
- **Full offline scanning:** Capture and process receipts without internet
- **Offline storage:** Receipts stored locally until connectivity returns
- **Automatic sync:** Seamless sync when connectivity is restored
- **No data loss:** Reliable storage ensures nothing is lost

### Exports That Actually Work

Your receipt data needs to flow into accounting software, spreadsheets, or tax preparation tools. Essential export features:
- **CSV export:** Universal compatibility with accounting software
- **Excel export:** Formatted spreadsheets ready for analysis
- **Custom fields:** Export the data fields you actually need
- **Batch export:** Export multiple receipts efficiently

### Pricing You Can Start With

Receipt scanning shouldn't require a financial commitment to evaluate. Look for:
- **Free to start:** Functional free tiers, not just teasers
- **Transparent pricing:** Clear costs without hidden fees
- **Reasonable limits:** Free tiers that allow real-world testing
- **Affordable upgrades:** Pricing that scales with actual usage
