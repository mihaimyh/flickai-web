## Tax receipts checklist

Stay organized for tax season.

Good receipt hygiene saves hours during tax season. Use this checklist to keep your receipts complete, searchable, and exportable.

## Checklist

- Capture merchant, date, total, and tax/VAT
- Add a category and a short note (client/project)
- Keep digital copies of important receipts
- Export monthly or quarterly for backups
- Separate personal vs. business spending

## FAQ

**What receipts should I keep?**
Keep receipts for business-related purchases and anything you plan to deduct.

**How long should I keep receipts?**
Rules vary by country — many people keep records for several years. Ask your accountant for local guidance.

**Can I export everything for my accountant?**
Yes — exports make it easier to share organized data and reduce back-and-forth.
