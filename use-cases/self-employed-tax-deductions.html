Track Self-Employed Tax Deductions

The easiest deductions are the ones you have receipts for. Build a clean record throughout the year.

What to track

  • Receipts (merchant, date, total).
  • Expense category and notes.
  • A searchable history you can export.

A simple workflow

  1. Scan and save receipts as you spend.
  2. Review categories and totals monthly.
  3. Export or share records at tax time.
