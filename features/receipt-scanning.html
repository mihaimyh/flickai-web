Smart Receipt Scanning

Gone are the days of manual data entry. With FlickAI, you can digitize expenses in seconds just by taking a photo.

How It Works

  1. Open the FlickAI app and tap the camera icon.
  2. Align the receipt in the frame.
  3. Tap capture. AI processes the image in seconds.
  4. Review the extracted details and save!

What We Extract

  • Merchant Name: Automatically identified from logos or text.
  • Date & Time: Records exactly when the purchase occurred.
  • Total Amount: Captures the final total accurately.
  • Individual Items: Extracts individual items and prices for detailed tracking.
