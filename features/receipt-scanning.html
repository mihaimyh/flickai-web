Smart Receipt Scanning

Gone are the days of manual data entry. With FlickAI, you can digitize your expenses in seconds just by taking a photo.

How It Works

  1. Open the FlickAI app and tap the camera icon.
  2. Align your receipt within the frame.
  3. Tap capture. The AI processes the image in seconds.
  4. Review the extracted details and save!

What We Extract

  • Merchant Name: Automatically identified from logos or text.
  • Date & Time: Records exactly when the purchase happened.
  • Total Amount: Captures the final total accurately.
  • Line Items: Extracts individual items and prices for detailed tracking.
