Offline expense tracking (checklist)

Offline tracking is about reliability: capture receipts and expenses immediately, even with no signal, then sync when you’re ready.

Checklist

  • Make sure capture works without an internet connection
  • Standardize categories (travel, meals, supplies, mileage)
  • Review totals weekly to catch mistakes early
  • Export backups for your accountant or records

FAQ

Why does offline mode matter?
It prevents lost receipts and missing entries when you’re traveling or have poor connectivity.

Is offline tracking private?
It can be — offline-first workflows reduce unnecessary data sharing and give you more control.

When should I sync?
Sync when it’s convenient and secure for you — for example, on trusted Wi‑Fi.
